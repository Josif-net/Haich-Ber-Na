JOSEPH BERNARD

Project

Modernising the Underground Man

Year

2021

Client

Personal

Discipline

Moving image & sound design

Modernising the Underground Man is an exploration of the 'incel' internet subculture. My aim was to highlight the similarities between the stereotypical characteristics of those within this subculture and the titular character in Fyodor Dostoevsky's 1821 novel 'Notes from Underground'. This project is comprised of a five minute video and related print matter.
