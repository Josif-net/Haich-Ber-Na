Where Did Everybody Go

Year

Client

Personal

Discipline

Moving image & sound design

Moving image project using found footage. 'Where Did Everybody Go' focuses on existential angst, using Camus' 'The Myth of Sisyphus' as a central reference point for the film. I stiched together various scenes with the aim of displaying a balance of hope and desperation. I combined an accapella and the original version of Bon Iver's 'Woods' to give an epic but measured pace.
