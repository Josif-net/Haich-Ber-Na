Identity & book design

Book design and related print matter from 'A Product of Windrush' - a project celebraating National Windrush Day. The book includes a series of photos shot in Carribbean communities throughout Greater-London, Birmingham, Liverpool and Leeds. The book is bound in a brown leather sleeve, a reference to the breifcases carried by the passangers aboard the HMT Empire Windrush.

As well as a book, I produced a poster featuring stamps I collected from all the Caribbean countries represented on board the ship.
