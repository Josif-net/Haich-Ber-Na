Project

Circular Sonics

Year

2020

Client

Personal

Discipline

Moving image & sound design

This film was made in response to a breif that asked me to reinterperate footage of Josef Albers teaching at Yale in 1955. As an outcome, the project could take on any medium. I chose moving image and sound design as a means to convey circles and loops in visual and audible formats.
