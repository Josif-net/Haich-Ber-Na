Project

Flower Power

Year

2022

Client

Earl of East

Discipline

Identity, product design, moving image

Earl of East's seasonal campaign inspired by the Hippie movement of the 1960s and 70s. I designed 9 products and / or the accompanying packaging, these included; glass incense holders and ashtrays, (produced by Batch Glass), water bottles, clothing, air fresheners, matches and chocolate.
