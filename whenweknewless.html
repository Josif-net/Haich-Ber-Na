Josif Bernard

Project

When We Knew Less

Year

2022

Client

Haich Ber Na

Discipline

Identity, product design

Brand identity assets and merchandise for Haich Ber Na's fourth EP, 'When We Knew Less', which is inspired by the fictional location of 'St. Leville'. The mascot for St. Leville, originally designed by Riku Hoshika and later reinterpreted by Josif Bernard, is a key element in the branding, merch, and website. As part of this project, I was responsible for creating keyring merchandise and designing the packaging.
