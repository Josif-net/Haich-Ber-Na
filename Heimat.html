JOSEPH BERNARD

Project

Reflections on "Heimat" - An Afro-German perspective

Year

2021

Client

Vogue Germany

Discipline

Sound design

I scored the video project for Vouge Germany's 'Heimat' (dir Delali Ayivi). Using a blend of tribal drums and swelling synthesizer I aimed to create an atmousphere of imbalance and cultural tension.

"Home" can evoke beautiful feelings, but is not a simple or innocent term. For many black people in Germany, "homeland" means a reality between familiarity and contesting one's own raison d'Ãªtre. It is about questioning visual connections with the concept of homeland and occupying them with new images. From the community, for the community. "Reflections to the homeland" illustrates the pride of belonging to a community that usually remains invisible in the context of German history and the present.
